## ‚ö° The Solution: The "FretCoach" Loop
We replaced the metronome with an intelligent feedback loop that engages your eyes, ears, and brain.

### 1. It Listens (The Audio Engine)
Our edge-based AI analyzes every single note of your scale run for:
* **Pitch Accuracy:** Did you bend that note slightly sharp?
* **Scale Conformity:** Did you hit a "forbidden" note outside the key?
* **Attack & Release:** are you letting notes bleed into each other?

### 2. It Visualizes (The Heatmap)
Forget standard sheet music. FretCoach highlights the fretboard on-screen:
* **Green:** Perfect pitch & timing.
* **Yellow:** You're rushing the beat.
* **Red:** Wrong note or fret buzz detected.

### 3. It Coaches (The Voice)
Instead of a beep, you get a coach:
> *"You're rushing the transition from the G to B string. Slow it down by 5 BPM and try again."*
